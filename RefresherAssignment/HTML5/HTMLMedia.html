Indian Ganpati festival

The festival of ganpati is celebrated every year throughout India especially in the West part of India. It is celebrated to pray Lord Ganesh, younger son of Lord Mahadeva(Shiva) and Godess Pravati. The Festival is celebrated with lot of zist and enthusiasm

This festival is of is celebrated for 10 days. During this festival everyone worships idol of lord Ganpati and serve various delicacies and sweets. Among them widely offered sweets are laddu.
